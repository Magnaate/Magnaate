Good morning one1, I trust you are in good spirits, and I thank you for your broad-brained comment.

Methinks that other Oracle Forum hunter gatherers will be interested in exploring the concept of 'assistance of the UNIVERSAL STAR' and its true definition further, certainly opined from your personal perspective, on its way to trigger the 'OPENING OF THE EYE' you refer to, an act which ultimately enables a human being to become a 'UNIVERSAL CITIZEN/SON OF GOD' (perchance these two should be separated as a fork on the 'road to achievement', as they are not one and the same, but dependent on faith and levels of belief?) YOUR VIEWS ON THE LATTER DIVISION would be also appreciated.

A brand new post on UNIVERSAL STAR and its most laudable definition, as opposed to the expression being used for a product rating system and by somebody that has the temerity to buy and sell stars commercially, would be much appreciated and a welcome contribution, oh one1 my friend.

Although from my own point of view, all stars become universal, on the premise that planetary life forms, including homo sapiens, are able to see, sometimes worship, dream on and above all enjoy them free of charge.

Indeed is there a more scientific bent to your observation? For example, are space 'wormholes', also known as Einstein–Rosen bridges the EYE you refer to, or do perhaps those full blooded, matter packed 'Black Holes' better fit the bill?

Does the fact that light finally reaches us from a distant star, mean that we in turn become more enlightened as the human race that inhabits earth, does it change our attitudes towards our fellows and soften extreme points of view, so that our minds are universally locked onto making life better for all?

It is said that for instance that the moon has a major influence on the moods and behavioural patterns of certain among us, hence the term lunatic, certainly owd Redbeck feels that influence. Although I grow neither fangs nor fur at full moon, but it has a depressive effect, I think. However, a carpet of stars unrolled above us on a clear night is one of the most exhilarating and spiritually uplifting free world experiences, inspiring feelings of romance, love and affection, assuming the effort of craning does not induce some painful infirmity as a result. "Would you be kind enough to unlock the vertebrae in my neck please?" he muttered to his female companion through clenched teeth.
